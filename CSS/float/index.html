Nature is the life-giving force that includes all living and non-living entities. The interconnectedness of these entities maintains the balance of our ecosystem. However, human activities like deforestation and industrial pollution are pushing nature to its limit, causing global warming and environmental degradation. It is now more important than ever to mitigate this damage by adopting sustainable practices like reusing and recycling. It is our collective responsibility to reduce the strain on our environment and restore its equilibrium. The ultimate goal must be to treat nature with the respect and care it deserves, to shield it from further harm.
